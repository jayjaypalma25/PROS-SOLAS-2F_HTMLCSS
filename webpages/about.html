what we provide?

At Howard and James Motor Shop, we provide a comprehensive range of automotive services using state-of-the-art tools and technology. Our offerings include routine maintenance such as oil changes, tire services, and fluid checks, all performed with high-quality materials and precision equipment. With hydraulic lifts, advanced tire balancers, and specialized performance tuning tools, we ensure every task is performed to the highest standard. Trust Howard and James Motor Shop for reliable, efficient, and top-quality automotive care.
