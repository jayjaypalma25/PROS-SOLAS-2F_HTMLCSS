Welcome to Howard and James Motor Shop

Welcome to Howard and James Motor Shop, your trusted destination for all your automotive needs. Established over 20 years ago by lifelong friends Howard and James, our shop is committed to providing high-quality, reliable, and affordable automotive services. Whether you need routine maintenance, complex repairs, or custom modifications, our team of experienced and certified technicians is here to help. We use state-of-the-art equipment to ensure your vehicle receives the best care possible. At Howard and James Motor Shop, we prioritize transparent communication, fair pricing, and exceptional customer service. Conveniently located with ample parking and a comfortable waiting area, we strive to make your experience as pleasant as possible. Join our community of satisfied customers and let us keep you safe on the road. Visit us today or contact us to schedule your next service!
